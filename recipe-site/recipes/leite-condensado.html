Home-made condensed milk

Condensed-milk is very tasty and used in many desserts, such as brigadeiros.

This recipe is described and provided by TUDOGOSTOSO.

Ingredients needed:

  • 1 tea cup of powder milk
  • 1 tea cup of sugar
  • half tea cup of boiling water

How to do

  1. Mix everything with the blender.
  2. Continue until it get consistancy
